My Name is Robert Maurice

I'm a UX/UI designer located in Austin, Texas with a passion for coding/development of websites and mobile apps. I have a BFA in Visual Communication from UTA and have been practicing design for several years. After graduating from UTA I moved to Japan to teach English for a year at a local taxi company, which is where the above image is from. Living in Japan had been a lifelong dream of mine so I jumped at the chance to finally accomplish it.

I relocated back to the United States in 2015 and have used my inspirations from my travels and meeting new people to design around human-centric products. During my free time, I enjoy playing video games, learning new programming languages, and reading about History.
